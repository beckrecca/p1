About Me

Hello! My name is Rebecca, and I live in Cambridge. I'm both a staff member and degree candidate at the Harvard Extension School. I am pursuing the ALM-IT degree with a major in Digital Media Arts and Instructional Design. I am particularly interested in web development, so I am pretty excited about this course! I spend the vast majority of my free time online, and it'd be awesome if I could make cool stuff for the interwebz.

Previously, I have studied Java with Dr. Leitner and some C and PHP with Dr. Malan here at the Extension School. As a teenager, I learned some HTML for fun and used to run my own blog back when Greymatter was a thing people used. I dropped that hobby for several years, but now that I'm back at it, I still really enjoy myself when I'm building a website. More recently, I've refreshed/updated my HTML and CSS skills in a couple of courses with Jen Kramer. Those courses also featured Bootstrap and a touch of JavaScript.

I have no professional experience programming. I find programming to be pretty absorbing and rewarding. Still, I often feel like AAHH WHAT AM I DOING I'M A FRAUD!! when coding. I think it's called Impostor Syndrome.

Oh, one last thing: I'm on a PC!
